Project Overview

Client: XXXX Group Corporation Research Institute XX, China


This system, based on virtual reality technology and integrating multi-channel interaction solutions, develops an innovative battlefield situation monitoring system with intelligent situation awareness at its core. The system integrates natural gesture recognition, somatosensory interaction, and eye tracking technologies to achieve real-time monitoring and intuitive presentation of battlefield situation information.

The system adopts a three-dimensional, interactive, and diversified presentation method, integrating advanced combat design concepts and innovative tactical elements. Through highly intuitive situation visualization technology, commanders can clearly grasp detailed geographic information and battlefield situations in real-time exercise or combat areas.
